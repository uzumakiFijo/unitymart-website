Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about UnityMart

How does the 3-month free trial work?
Sign up for any paid plan (Basic, Pro, or Premium) and get 3 months completely free. No credit card required. After 3 months, you can choose to continue with monthly billing or cancel anytime with no penalty.
How quickly can I get started?
Setup takes just 5-10 minutes! Fill out a simple form with your store details, upload a few photos, and you're live. We'll help you every step of the way. Most stores are up and running the same day.
Do I need technical skills?
Not at all! If you can use WhatsApp, you can use UnityMart. Our dashboard is simple and intuitive. Plus, we provide free training and support to help you get the most out of the platform.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. If you upgrade, you'll get access to new features immediately. If you downgrade, changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
How do customers find my store?
Customers download the free UnityMart app and search for "Indian stores near me." Your store appears on the map with all your details. They can filter by features like fresh meat, halal certification, or delivery availability.
What if I have multiple store locations?
The Premium plan (‚Ç¨99/month) includes multi-location management. You can manage all your stores from one dashboard, with separate listings, offers, and analytics for each location.
How do I update fresh meat availability?
Simply log into your dashboard and toggle "Fresh Meat Available" to ON. Customers who favorited your store get an instant notification. You can also specify what types of meat are available (chicken, mutton, fish, etc.).
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, absolutely! There are no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with one click from your dashboard. Your listing will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and Revolut. Payments are processed securely through Stripe. You'll receive an invoice via email each month.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees, no hidden charges! The price you see is the price you pay. We believe in transparent, simple pricing. What you see is what you get.
